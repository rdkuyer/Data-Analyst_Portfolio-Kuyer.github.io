End-to-End Videogame Analysis SQL/Power BI

I analyzed the videogame sales of Nintendo, PlayStation, and Xbox, which collectively account for 90% of the total market share. I focused on the Japanese market, providing insights into Xbox's limited penetration in this market.

Cleaning Housing Data Python/SQL

Plant Co Sales Performance report

I created a sales analysis report comparing Year-to-Date (YTD) and Prior Year-to-Date (PYTD) sales for a fictitious plant company. This report helps identify key drivers behind the decrease in sales and highlights the most potentially profitable accounts.
